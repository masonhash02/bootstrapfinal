Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team

Mercedes returned as a full works team in 2010 and quickly rose to dominate the turbo-hybrid era, setting records for consecutive constructors’ and drivers’ championships.

  • Founded: 2010 (works team, origins in 1954)
  • Base: Brackley, United Kingdom
  • World Constructors’ Titles: 8
  • World Drivers’ Titles: 9
  • Notable Drivers: Lewis Hamilton, Nico Rosberg, George Russell
  • Current Drivers: George Russell, Andrea Kimi Antonelli

Scuderia Ferrari

The oldest and most iconic team in Formula 1, Ferrari has raced in every season since the championship began in 1950. Known for their passionate fanbase and legendary cars.

  • Founded: 1929 (F1 debut 1950)
  • Base: Maranello, Italy
  • World Constructors’ Titles: 16
  • World Drivers’ Titles: 15
  • Notable Drivers: Michael Schumacher, Niki Lauda, Charles Leclerc
  • Current Drivers: Charles Leclerc, Lewis Hamilton

Red Bull Racing

Red Bull entered Formula 1 in 2005 and quickly became a powerhouse, known for their innovative design and aggressive race strategies. They’ve been the team to beat in the 2020s.

  • Founded: 2005
  • Base: Milton Keynes, United Kingdom
  • World Constructors’ Titles: 6
  • World Drivers’ Titles: 7
  • Notable Drivers: Sebastian Vettel, Max Verstappen
  • Current Drivers: Max Verstappen, Yuki Tsunoda

McLaren F1 Team

McLaren is one of F1’s most successful and historic teams, famous for innovation and legendary champions. They continue to compete at the sharp end of the grid.

  • Founded: 1963 (F1 debut 1966)
  • Base: Woking, United Kingdom
  • World Constructors’ Titles: 8
  • World Drivers’ Titles: 12
  • Notable Drivers: Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, Fernado Alonso
  • Current Drivers: Lando Norris, Oscar Piastri
